GOALS

We aim to nurture a pool of skilled manpower to further develop Singapore as a Smart Financial Centre.

We seek to work with the FinTech community locally and globally to provide internships and mentoring opportunities to polytechnic students who are keen to pursue careers in FinTech, helping them understand markets, technology, skills, ecosystem players and regulatory environments.

MENTORSHIP

We welcome experienced professionals to register as mentors to polytechnic students who are keen to build careers in FinTech. We plan to build a community of at least 100 FinTech mentors for the polytechnic sector in Singapore.

 
View More

INTERNSHIP

We welcome companies to register their interest to host polytechnic interns for FinTech roles locally or overseas. These internships typically run for 4-6 months in the months of March to September and October to April each year. The matching of companies and interns begins in January and July or earlier for overseas internships. We plan to start with at least 100 internships in 2017.
