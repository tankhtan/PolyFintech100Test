INTERNSHIP

We welcome companies to register their interest to host polytechnic interns for FinTech roles locally or overseas. These internships typically run for 4-6 months in the months of March to September and October to April each year. The matching of companies and interns begins in January and July or earlier for overseas internships. We plan to start with at least 100 internships in 2017.
